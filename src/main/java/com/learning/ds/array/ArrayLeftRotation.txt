Types of Rotations In Array
------------------------------
	1. Right Rotation
	---------------------------------------------
		1 2 3 4 5 6 -> 6 1 2 3 4 5 -> 5 6 1 2 3 4
	
	2. Left Rotation
	----------------------------------------------
		1 2 3 4 5 6 -> 2 3 4 5 6 1 -> 3 4 5 6 1 2

1st Approach
---------------------------------------------------------------------
	-> Store the first element in a temporary variable.
	-> Shift the rest of the elements in array by one place.
	-> Update the last index of the array with temporary variable.
